Character Profile

My character's name is Azura Enigma. She is 20 years old, is 4 feet 8 inches tall, and weights 110 pounds. Quite small and light for a human, even one this young. She also possesses brown hair, olive skin, and normally has green eyes, just like what is shown in this illustration. However, when she is recieving a vision, her eyes glow blue. In most circumstances she wields either her wooden staff as a magic focal, or a scimitar and shield, and she always wears owl feathers in her hair and a necklace with the symbol of an unknown goddess on it.
